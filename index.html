About the PGL

We at PGL have been gamers all our life, but we have finally hit the point where there is just very little time in the day for gaming. The PGL was born out of necessity for people who want to game at a competitive level, but cannot meet the rigorous timetable that most gaming leagues require.

By scheduling gaming on a weekly basis, people can deconfict their gaming around work, spouses, children and other hobbies.

We love to game, but as we grew older, we lost the group of friends that we played with, and finding new ones is nearly impossible. At the PGL we help provide the team, and the league so that everyone can play in a friendly competitive environment.

    All statistics, for every game are recorded and visible on league leaderboards! All matches are reviewed for accuracy. Later API implementation will allow for near realtime statistics.
